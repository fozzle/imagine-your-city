I Wish This Was, Where? was built during Random Hacks of Kindness in June 2012 by Kyle Petrovich, Jeff Walters, and David Emory and spawned from the idea of Mathilde Piard, piggybacking on artist Candy Chang's I Wish This Was public art project that invites residents to provide civic input on-site.

To join the fun, get some "I wish this was" stickers (ATLiens can get them for free) and post your photos to Instagram with the hashtag #iwishthiswas - make sure geotagging is turned on so your photo shows up on the map!

Future ideas for the site include being able to comment and upvote project ideas, possibly links to related Kickstarters, and more!
